Project

ChattyPatty

Distributed compute AI network In the lab
Visit ChattyPatty Contact

ChattyPatty is a distributed AI compute network where people share idle laptops and desktops to run AI jobs for others. It routes workloads, measures usage, and lowers cost for teams without high-end hardware.

What It Solves

  • Runs jobs on idle machines without heavy setup.
  • Routes workloads and tracks usage.
  • Cuts the cost of model runs for small teams.

Status

Prototype stage with early node onboarding and routing tests.

Independent software builder working on local-first and privacy-first systems.
