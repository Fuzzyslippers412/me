Project

ChattyPatty

Distributed compute AI marketplace In the lab
Visit ChattyPatty Contact

ChattyPatty is a distributed AI compute marketplace where people share idle laptop and desktop power to run AI models for others. It routes workloads, measures usage, and makes access to AI cheaper for users without high-end hardware.

What It Solves

  • Lets people monetize idle compute without complex setup.
  • Routes AI workloads to the right nodes and tracks usage.
  • Lowers the cost of running models for teams without GPUs.

Status

Prototype stage with early node onboarding and workload routing tests.
