Visit MyCasaPro Contact

Local-first home operations system with a Next.js UI and FastAPI backend. LTI multi-agent teams (manager, finance, maintenance, security, contractors, projects) coordinate via scheduling and approvals, backed by a SecondBrain knowledge vault, user-owned connectors, and built-in Railgun private payments.

Who It Serves

  • Homeowners and small teams managing home operations locally.
  • Operators who need offline-first control and fast local setup.
  • Teams that require private payments and agent-driven workflows.

Core Capabilities

  • Next.js UI + FastAPI backend with dashboards, inbox, and approvals.
  • Multi-agent teams with scheduling, routing, and workflow coordination.
  • SecondBrain knowledge vault for durable memory and context.
  • User-owned connectors (Gmail, WhatsApp, Calendar) and built-in Railgun private payments.

Status

Active build with multi-agent teams, SecondBrain memory, approvals and scheduling, and built-in Railgun private payments.
