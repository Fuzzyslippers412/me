GhostProtocol

Privacy-first coordination Verifiable routing Exploration
Visit GhostProtocol Contact

GhostProtocol is a privacy-first coordination layer for teams that need verifiable routing, minimal metadata exposure, and resilient communication. It is designed for environments where trust, auditability, and latency matter.

What It Solves

  • Secure routing without leaking participant metadata.
  • Durable coordination across unstable networks.
  • Proof-ready logs for compliance and auditing.

Status

Research and architecture exploration with a focus on privacy guarantees.
