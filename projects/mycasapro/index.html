Visit MyCasaPro Contact

MyCasaPro is a local-first coordination and verification platform for homeowners and vendors. It tracks milestones, evidence, approvals, and payments with a tamper-evident event ledger and optional private payment linkage.

Who It Serves

  • Homeowners who need trustworthy milestone tracking and proof of completion.
  • Vendors and contractors who want fast, verifiable payment workflows.
  • Teams managing multiple jobs across locations and time zones.

Core Capabilities

  • Local-first project data with conflict-free sync.
  • Milestone verification with cryptographic evidence trails.
  • Payment routing designed for privacy-preserving proofs.
  • Audit-ready logs for compliance and dispute resolution.

Status

Active development with a focus on milestone verification flows and private payment routing. Pilot partners are welcome.
