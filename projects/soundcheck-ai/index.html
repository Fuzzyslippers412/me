Soundcheck.AI

Financial filings Spending verification Active
Visit Soundcheck.AI Contact

Soundcheck.AI uses AI to analyze public nonprofit and school financial filings (including IRS records) to surface spending patterns and verify whether organizations spend money the way they claim.

What It Solves

  • Flags spending patterns that diverge from public claims.
  • Summarizes IRS and school filing data for fast review.
  • Creates evidence trails for donors, boards, and auditors.

Status

Active build focused on filing ingestion and verification summaries.

Independent software builder focused on verifiable systems and privacy-preserving infrastructure.
