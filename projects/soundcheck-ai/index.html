Soundcheck.AI

Audio verification Model evaluation Active
Visit Soundcheck.AI Contact

Soundcheck.AI uses AI to analyze public nonprofit and school financial filings (including IRS records) to surface spending patterns and verify whether organizations spend money the way they claim. It focuses on measurable quality baselines and repeatable audio acceptance tests.

What It Solves

  • Objective baselines for audio model performance.
  • Regression detection before releases.
  • Clear reporting for production readiness.
