Theo.farm

Local-first operations Active Founder and builder

Theo.farm is a local-first operations platform for growers and small farms to coordinate schedules, inventory, and field data without losing control. It is designed to work offline, sync safely, and keep operational records verifiable.

Who It Serves

  • Growers who need consistent operations tracking across seasons.
  • Teams coordinating labor, supply, and field schedules.
  • Operators who require durable, privacy-respecting data ownership.

Status

Active build with a focus on offline-first workflows and field verification.
